Ceilings

We offer a wide range of suspended ceilings installation, from advice and free site surveys and quotations to full tailor-made ceiling systems for homes and offices.

BULKHEADS

We build bulkheads with 9mm Rhino Gypsum board and galvanized light steel material to define certain areas or to create different levels between horizontal ceiling planes.

INSULATION

Insulation is installed on top of the ceiling to keep homes and offices cool in summer and warm in winter. Evolwent’s team makes use of Isotherm isolation.

DRYWALL

Our drywall solutions use 12mm Rhino boards that are screwed against the light steel frame. After installation of boards, joints are skimmed for a perfect finish.
